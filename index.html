About Me

I am a JSPS researcher at TU Wien (The Set Theory Group) since April 2025. My host researcher is Jakob Kellner. I am interested in set theory of reals, especially in cardinal invariants and their applications to other areas of mathematics. In particular, I have recently been interested in applications to general topology.

I was born in Kobe in Japan and have been affiliated with various Japanese universities during my school years. My undergraduate studies were at Ehime University and Tsukuba University, my master's at Nagoya University, and my doctoral at Kobe University. This is how I have been doing "random walks" in Japan, but suddenly I decided to fly out and do research in Vienna :)
